About Us

Asta Cafe is a haven for food enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. Our cafe is strategically located in the picturesque mountains of Himachal, offering breathtaking views of the sunset with paragliders soaring in the sky.

At Asta Cafe, we believe in delivering an extraordinary culinary experience that elevates your spirit while delighting your palate. Whether you're here to unwind after a thrilling paragliding session or simply to immerse yourself in the serene surroundings, we welcome you with open arms.

Menu

Our menu is carefully crafted to offer a blend of local and international flavors. From traditional Himachali delicacies to global favorites, each dish is prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients to ensure the highest quality and taste.

Be it a refreshing beverage to quench your thirst or a scrumptious meal to satisfy your hunger, our menu has something to please every discerning palate. We take pride in providing an unforgettable dining experience to all our guests.
